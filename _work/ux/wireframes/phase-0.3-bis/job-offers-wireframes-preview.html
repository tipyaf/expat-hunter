Senior Frontend Engineer

Publié le 08 jan 2025 · Fermeture : 31 jan 2025   Voir sur Seek ↗ Voir sur LinkedIn ↗
$120k – $140k NZD

Description de l'offre

We're looking for a Senior Frontend Engineer to join our product team at Acme Corp. You'll be working on our flagship SaaS platform used by thousands of businesses across New Zealand and Australia.


Requirements:

  • 8+ years of experience with React/TypeScript
  • Experience with Next.js and modern build tools
  • Strong understanding of web performance
  • Experience mentoring junior developers

Nice to have:

  • Experience with Node.js backend
  • Knowledge of cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP)

7. Espace de candidature — CV + Lettre + Email

Page la plus complexe : split 50/50 (CV gauche, lettre droite) + footer email

← Retour a l'offre Candidature : Senior Frontend @ Acme Corp

📄 CV adapte

Remplacements IA (5/7 max) :

5 ans d'experience en developpement web → 8 ans d'experience React/TypeScript en environnement SaaS
Developpeur Frontend → Senior Frontend Engineer & Tech Lead
Maitrise de JavaScript et CSS → Expert TypeScript, React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS
Projets web varies → Plateformes SaaS B2B a fort trafic (100k+ utilisateurs)
Travail en equipe → Leadership technique : mentoring de 4 devs juniors, code reviews

✉️ Lettre de motivation

Dear Hiring Team,


I am writing to express my strong interest in the Senior Frontend Engineer position at Acme Corp. With over 8 years of experience building SaaS platforms using React and TypeScript, I am confident I can make a significant contribution to your product team.


Throughout my career, I have led frontend teams in delivering high-performance web applications serving over 100,000 users. My experience with Next.js, modern build tools, and performance optimization aligns closely with your requirements.


I am particularly drawn to Acme Corp's commitment to innovation in the New Zealand tech ecosystem. Having recently relocated to Auckland, I am eager to contribute to a growing company with strong engineering culture.


I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience can benefit your team.


Kind regards,
Yannick Bouvier
